## Oinkoin

{{< rawhtml >}}
<img style="float: left; margin-right: 20px;" src="/images/oinkoin.png" width="100" />
{{< /rawhtml >}}

Oinkoin is a cross-platform personal finance and expense-tracking application built with Flutter. Manage your personal finances easily and securely with just few taps. It is an offline and ad-free app. (390+ stars, ~1400 monthly active users)

## JustBooth

{{< rawhtml >}}
<img style="float: left; margin-right: 20px;" src="/images/justbooth.png" width="100" />
{{< /rawhtml >}}

Turn any Android tablet or phone into a standalone photobooth — no extra hardware, no complicated setup. Works with the built-in camera or Olympus cameras via Wi-Fi. Features many configuration options, among which: kiosk mode, support for any printing app, branding, and more.
- [JustBooth on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=eu.viglianisi.photobooth)
- [Blog post](/posts/photo-booth-for-wedding/)
